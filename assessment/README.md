# Assessment: SCI for AI Fundamentals

## Overview

The course assessment consists of 12 questions covering the full arc of the course: why the SCI was designed the way it was, how the equation works, the difference between Provider and Consumer responsibilities, choosing the right functional unit, aligning accountability with agency, and making smart decisions with the data you have.

**Pass mark:** 75% (9 out of 12 correct)

**Reward:** [SCI for AI Fundamentals digital badge](badge/)

The assessment is hosted on the course platform. The questions and correct answers are documented in [quiz.md](quiz.md) for transparency and to support the CC-BY 4.0 licence.

## Digital Badge

Score 75% or above and you'll earn the **SCI for AI Fundamentals** digital badge — a shareable credential that demonstrates your ability to measure and reduce the carbon emissions of AI systems.

See [badge/README.md](badge/README.md) for full details.
